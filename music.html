King Me

On the bold track, Bobby V makes major declarations about his contributions to music. He even cites politics as the reason he doesn’t have a Grammy. One of the more bombastic moments of the song comes in quickly after his snub claim. “Ain’t never had a hand-me-down/ Aborting these dollars like Plan B,” he states as he smoothly rides the beat.

Electrik

R&B changed its sound over the years, with newer artists and genre categories becoming obsolete. Bobby V marks this new album as “the return of real R&B,” adding “I refuse to let the beautiful craft of R&B die.”
